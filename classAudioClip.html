◆ assignDevice()

void AudioClip::assignDevice ( AudioDevice  device)

Assigns an AudioDevice through which this clip will play.

Remarks
If an AudioMixer was assigned to this clip, that AudioMixer 's assigned AudioDevice will also be assigned to this clip. Programmer cannot override a clip's AudioDevice if that clip is connected to an AudioMixer ; to do that the clip's AudioMixer should be assigned to nullptr .
Parameters
deviceAudioDevice where the clip is desired to play.

◆ assignMixer()

void AudioClip::assignMixer ( AudioMixer *  mixer)

Assigns an AudioMixer to this clip.

Remarks
Every change made to the AudioMixer will now affect this clip. If the clip was already assigned to another AudioMixer the changes will be overwritten by the new ones.
Parameters
mixerAudioMixer to assign to the clip.

Updates the clip's global playing volume depending on the clip's volume and on it's AudioMixer 's if it has any assigned.

Remarks
